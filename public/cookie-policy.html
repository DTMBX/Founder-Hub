Cookie Policy

Effective Date: June 8, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how devon-tyler.com (the "Site"), operated by Devon Tyler Barber through Evident Technologies LLC and Tillerstead LLC, uses cookies and similar technologies.

1. We Do Not Use Cookies

The Site does not set any cookies — first-party or third-party. This means:

  • No tracking cookies.
  • No analytics cookies.
  • No advertising or retargeting cookies.
  • No session cookies.
  • No authentication cookies (the public site has no user accounts).

Because we do not use cookies, no cookie consent banner is required.

2. Local Storage

The Site uses your browser's localStorage API to save user preferences such as theme settings and display options. localStorage is different from cookies:

  • Data is stored only on your device.
  • Data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.
  • Data persists until you clear your browser storage or the Site removes it.
  • Data is not accessible to other websites.

The following localStorage keys may be used:

Key PatternPurposePersistence
themeDark/light mode preferenceUntil cleared
founder-hub-admin:*Admin dashboard settings (admin use only)Until cleared

3. Third-Party Cookies

We do not embed any third-party services that set cookies. Specifically:

  • No Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, or Google Ads.
  • No Meta (Facebook) Pixel or social media tracking.
  • No embedded third-party iframes that set cookies.
  • No Cloudflare challenge cookies (our Cloudflare configuration does not require browser challenges).

4. Infrastructure-Level Cookies

Our hosting providers (GitHub Pages, Cloudflare) may set technical cookies at the infrastructure level for security purposes (such as DDoS protection). These are outside our control and are governed by their respective privacy policies:

  • GitHub Privacy Statement
  • Cloudflare Privacy Policy

5. Managing Browser Storage

You can clear localStorage data at any time through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Clear browsing data → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Clear Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Clear browsing data

Clearing localStorage will reset any saved preferences (such as theme choice) but will not affect your ability to use the Site.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we ever introduce cookies in the future, we will update this policy, implement appropriate consent mechanisms, and notify users through visible site updates.
