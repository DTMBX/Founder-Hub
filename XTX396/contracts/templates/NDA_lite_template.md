## 2. Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means all non-public information disclosed by
Discloser to Receiver, whether oral, written, electronic, or visual, that is
designated as confidential or that a reasonable person would understand to be
confidential given the nature of the information.

Confidential Information does not include information that:

(a) Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of Receiver  
(b) Was already known to Receiver prior to disclosure  
(c) Is independently developed by Receiver without use of Confidential Information  
(d) Is rightfully received from a third party without restriction  

## 3. Obligations

3.1. Receiver shall use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose.  
3.2. Receiver shall limit access to individuals with a need to know.  
3.3. Receiver shall protect Confidential Information with at least the same
degree of care it uses for its own confidential information.

## 4. Term

## 5. Return of Materials

Information and certify destruction in writing.

## 6. Remedies

may seek equitable relief in addition to other remedies.
