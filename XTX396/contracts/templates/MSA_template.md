## 1. Scope of Agreement

This Master Service Agreement ("Agreement") establishes the general terms and
conditions under which Provider will deliver services to Client. Specific
engagements are defined in individual Statements of Work ("SOW") executed
pursuant to this Agreement.

## 2. Term

This Agreement is effective as of {{EFFECTIVE_DATE}} and continues for
{{INITIAL_TERM}} unless terminated per Section 10.

## 3. Services

Provider will perform services as described in each SOW. Each SOW will specify:

- Scope of work
- Deliverables
- Timeline
- Fees and payment terms
- Acceptance criteria

## 4. Fees and Payment

4.1. Fees are specified in the applicable SOW.  
4.2. Payment terms: Net {{PAYMENT_TERMS_DAYS}} from invoice date.  
4.3. Late payments accrue interest at {{LATE_INTEREST_RATE}} per month.

## 5. Confidentiality

5.1. Each party agrees to maintain confidential information in strict
confidence.  
5.2. Confidential information excludes information that is: (a) publicly
available, (b) independently developed, (c) received from a third party
without restriction, or (d) required to be disclosed by law.  
5.3. Obligations survive termination for {{CONFIDENTIALITY_TERM}}.

## 6. Intellectual Property

6.1. Client retains ownership of all Client data and materials provided to
Provider.  
6.2. Work product created under a SOW is owned by {{IP_OWNER}} upon full
payment.  
6.3. Provider retains rights to pre-existing intellectual property, tools,
and methodologies.

## 7. Data Handling

7.1. Provider will handle Client data in accordance with applicable privacy
laws and the data handling provisions specified in the SOW.  
7.2. Provider will implement reasonable security measures to protect Client
data.  
7.3. Upon termination, Provider will return or destroy Client data per
Client's instructions.

## 8. Warranties

8.1. Provider warrants that services will be performed in a professional and

8.2. EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY SET FORTH HEREIN, PROVIDER MAKES NO WARRANTIES,
EXPRESS OR IMPLIED.

9.2. Provider's total liability shall not exceed {{LIABILITY_CAP}}.

## 10. Termination

written notice.  
10.2. Either party may terminate immediately for material breach not cured
within {{CURE_PERIOD}}.
